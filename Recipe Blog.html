  1. Spaghetti Carbonara:

    Ingredients: Spaghetti Pancetta or bacon Eggs Parmesan cheese Black pepper Instructions: Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. In a separate pan, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. Drain spaghetti and add it to the pan with pancetta/bacon. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture until the eggs thicken and coat the pasta. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese and black pepper.

  2. Homemade Margherita Pizza:

    Ingredients: Pizza dough Tomato sauce Fresh mozzarella cheese Fresh basil leaves Olive oil Instructions: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out pizza dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough. Tear fresh mozzarella into small pieces and distribute evenly over the sauce. Arrange fresh basil leaves on top of the cheese. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.

  3. Chicken Alfredo Pasta:

    Ingredients: Fettuccine pasta Chicken breast Heavy cream Butter Garlic Parmesan cheese Salt and pepper Instructions: Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Season chicken breast with salt and pepper, then grill or sauté until fully cooked. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat and sauté minced garlic until fragrant. Pour in heavy cream and let it simmer until slightly thickened. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Slice cooked chicken breast and add it to the sauce. Toss cooked pasta in the creamy Alfredo sauce until well coated. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper.
